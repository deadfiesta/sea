  • Introducing the 3 'S'es

  • Understanding SeaTalk business goals for their consumers

  • Simplifying into 3 keywords by deconstructing SeaTalk’s slogan

    "One-stop for work"

    • Simplicity

      Approachable user design with easy-to-use functionalities.

    • Seamless

      Internal integrations within the app and some degree of external integration with third-party platforms.

    • Sustainable

      Save time and money so businesses can focus and invest more resources to iron out business operations for stability and permanence.

    • Effortlessness

      How easy was it to use?

      The need for SeaTalk to be as intuitive as possible is essential as our target audience are average tech consumers who may not be “app-savvy”. The last thing we want is to intimidate our users and drop our app at first sight. Effortlessness should have the highest weight. We want to welcome and motivate our users to dive right in with a design that they’re familiar with. A friendly interface that requires very little guidance or learning curve.

    • Effectiveness

      How well of a job did it do?

      Features in SeaTalk should add value whenever possible. This means giving the users an end result better than their desired expectation of the action performed.

    • Efficiency

      How fast to get the job done?

      The nature of any productivity app like SeaTalk should assist users to accomplish more but with less work in less time. Every action should be seamless; simplified and autonomous, cutting down as many steps as possible.
